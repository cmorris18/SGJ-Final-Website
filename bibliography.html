A Note on Sources

The two encyclopedia entries from the Jewish Women's Archive (JWA) provide the biographical foundation for the profiles of Charlotte Epstein and Andrea Gyarmati.

The two articles by Linda J. Borish are the primary scholarly foundation for the historical and institutional analysis in this project. Borish is a historian of American women's sport with particular expertise in Jewish women's athletic history. Her 2004 article focuses on Epstein specifically; her 1999 article provides the broader context of Jewish women's sport participation across six decades.

Ofer Idels's 2022 article in the Journal of Modern Jewish Studies provides the ideological framework of "Muscular Judaism" and the "New Jew" paradox, which this project uses to situate Epstein and Gyarmati within a broader Jewish cultural politics of the body and athletic identity.
